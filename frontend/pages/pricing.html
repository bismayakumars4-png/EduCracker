❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the Free plan?

Free users get 2 AI notes and 1 mock test per day, basic dashboard, and access to the community leaderboard. Perfect for trying out the platform!

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! You can cancel your Pro subscription anytime. You'll continue to have access until your billing period ends, and your data is never deleted.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, Paytm, Google Pay, and PhonePe. All payments are secured with encrypted transactions.

Is there a refund policy?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied, contact support for a full refund - no questions asked!

What happens when my free limit ends?

When you reach your daily limit, you'll see an upgrade prompt. Your data is always saved - just upgrade to continue without interruptions!

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes! Contact us with your student ID for a special 50% discount on Pro plan. We believe education should be accessible to everyone.
